Overview

\n

Arash, the legendary archer of Iranian mythology, is one of the best Servants for farming and clearing waves. His Noble Phantasm is both easy to level up to 5 and insanely powerful to boot. As an Area of Effect NP, it can reliably kill a wave of enemies in daily missions (with the exception of Lancer enemies) in one go, with the caveat of Arash dying upon using it. A team fielding Arash usually has him started with a CE which increases starting NP charge, along with support Servants who can charge his NP as well, so that he can fire it off as soon as possible.

\n

Though seemingly detrimental, Arash\xe2\x80\x99s death upon NP use can actually be useful as it allows for another Servant to replace him immediately. His death can\xc2\xa0even be used in a combo with certain CEs that have an effect on death. However, apart from his very strong NP, Arash lacks the stats and the skills to impact battles. As such, it\xe2\x80\x99s recommended that he keeps to his farming/burst damage niche. All in all, Arash is definitely a Servant that should be raised even just for his sheer usefulness in farming.

\n

Strengths

\n
\n

One of the Best Servants for Farming

\n

Arash\xe2\x80\x99s AoE NP can be used to clear almost any wave (except Lancers) during any of the daily ember/QP quests, while killing him in the process. However, the fact that Arash dies can actually be a boon as it allows another Servant from your backline to instantly replace him without having to use the\xc2\xa0Order Change\xc2\xa0ability from the Chaldea Combat Uniform. Additionally, as he is a 1-Star Servant, he only takes up 3 cost from the party. As a result, a Master can\xc2\xa0place other more higher rarity Servants in their farming team (for bond point farming).

\n

Death upon NP Use Combo Potential

\n

Outside of farming, Arash can also be used in normal battles as a big damage nuke followed by another Servant replacing him. This allows for some nice combos with Craft Essences like 500-Year Obsession which gives beneficial effects for the team upon death, as well as immediately getting another Servant out to battle.

\n
\n

Weaknesses

\n
\n

Limited Use Outside of Niche

\n

Having low star generation, stats and a vanilla skill set make Arash less than ideal for battles outside of his niche. He is best used as a one-time high damage burst followed by other strategies. Thus, building a team around Arash is not recommended.

\n

Death upon NP Use Detrimental in Tougher Battles

\n

Arash\xe2\x80\x99s NP is a double-edged sword. Aside from being able to deal a high damage nuke and his resulting death being able to combo into other strategies, using Arash in such a\xc2\xa0manner would still leave you one Servant down overall. Thus, a Master should be careful when using Arash during tougher battles. Note that when Arash uses his NP while he is the last Servant standing, the battle will be considered a loss even if\xc2\xa0the enemy perished.

\n

\n
\n
\n
  • Kaleidoscope / Imaginary Element: The definitive CE equip for Arash. It allows for instant NPs\xc2\xa0with Arrow Construction for quick battles. The faster Arash can fire off his NP, the better.\xc2\xa0
    • \n
  • Devilish Bodhisattva\xc2\xa0/\xc2\xa0Golden Sumo / Holy Night Supper:\xc2\xa0Sometimes Arash needs a slightly bigger boost to his damage to fully clear a wave. Combined with Servants like Zhuge Liang, Arash can still fire off his NP instantly despite the reduced Starting NP Gauge. Arash scales very well with Overcharge, making Devilish Bodhisattva an excellent option.\xc2\xa0
    • \n
  • Sacred Devotion / 500-Year Obsession / The Merciless One: If there already are other Servants like Zhuge Liang who can charge Arash\xe2\x80\x99s NP, having a CE which gives beneficial effects upon death can be useful in battles against tough enemies. Arash\'s Bond CE is particularly strong for this purpose.\xc2\xa0

Character Info

\n

The legendary hero of ancient Persia.
\nAs a warrior for King Manuchehr, who is dubbed the final king of ancient West Asia, he ended the war between the Persians and Turks that lasted 60 years.
\nHe is the hero of salvation that gave both peoples peace and tranquility.

\n
\n
\n

Profile 1

\n

Height/Weight: 185cm, 75kg
\nOrigin: Persian Mythology
\nArea: West Asia
\nAlignment: Chaotic-Balanced
\nGender: Male
\nThe skill Toughness includes anti-poison and improved resistance.

\n
\n
\n

Profile 2

\n

Nicknamed Arash Kamangir, which translates into "Arash the Archer" in English.
\nIn Western Asian nations, Arash has become the symbol of the peace-bringing archers.
\nEven now, he is loved by many people in Western Asia.

[Stella]
\nRank: B++
\nNP Type: Anti-Army
\nThe Streaking Meteor. The ultimate shot to end all battles.

Profile 4

In legends, Arash used this ultimate shot to create a "border" between Persia and Turan.
