Overview

\n

\xe2\x80\x9cIf I try to focus on something, it would appear as if it was drawn on a piece of paper. Then finally, I pick up that \'paper\' and start folding it.\xe2\x80\x9d

\n

A wielder of the \xe2\x80\x98Mystic Eyes of Distortion\xe2\x80\x99, Asagami Fujino is an Archer-class Servant featured in the Kara no Kyoukai/the Garden of Order [Revival] event, whose kit boasts various utilities as well as high survivability, with a Buster-type single-target NP as her primary source of damage.

\n

The most notable part of Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s kit is the three utility tools it contains, namely defense ignore from Mystic Eyes of Distortion (Skill), sure hit from Clairvoyance (Darkness), and a buff block from Yuishiki - Waikyoku no Magan. Skillful use of these tools allows her to bypass the gimmicks of various bosses, making her an excellent choice for difficult quests. Added to this are her impressive survivability \xe2\x80\x94 courtesy of Residual Pain and her NP\xe2\x80\x99s attack down debuff \xe2\x80\x94 as well as the natural critical compatibility of her Archer class. The latter, combined with Mystic Eyes of Distortion\xe2\x80\x99s NP gain buff and Territory Creation B, improve on her NP gain and bringing it up to above average levels.

\n

\xc2\xa0Asagami Fujino, however, has one major weakness, and that is an issue of availability, which will be covered further below. As for her kit itself, while Residual Pain\xe2\x80\x99s damage reduction works very well against normal hits and comes with a 1-HP Guts, Fujino is still highly vulnerable to enemy bosses\xe2\x80\x99 heavy-hitting criticals and NPs. Her NP Damage is also lower than might be expected, owing to her lower-magnitude damage boost (though it lasts for 3 turns) and unupgraded NP.

\n

Overlooking her low availability, Fujino is a very survivable Archer damage dealer who fares well against the gimmicks often found in difficult quests. Barring the most heavy of assaults, she generally requires only a small amount of healing support to sustain herself, and careful utilization of her tools can save Masters a lot of effort when facing gimmick-heavy bosses.

\n

Strengths

\n
\n

High Utility

\n

Mystic Eyes of Distortion\xe2\x80\x99s ignore defense and Clairvoyance (Darkness)\xe2\x80\x99s sure hit together already allow Fujino to bypass half of the defensive measures available in-game, but her NP\xe2\x80\x99s buff block brings her utility to another level. When used under the right timing (which is rather easy owing to its 3-turn duration), it can potentially deny powerful effects such as damage boosts, invincibility/evasion, as well as invincibility ignore/sure hit.

\n

Impressive Survivability

\n

Despite Residual Pain\xe2\x80\x99s 2000 Max HP demerit, its damage reducing effect more than makes up for it. With a 60% uptime at max skill level, it effectively nullifies the damage of most enemy mobs, and with a bit of external defense buffs (along with possibly Yuishiki - Waikyoku no Magan\xe2\x80\x99s attack down debuff), she can even shrug off bosses\xe2\x80\x99 attacks with little problem. Additionally, the skill\xe2\x80\x99s Guts effect provides an extra layer of protection should something go wrong.

\n

Decent NP Gain and Critical Compatibility

\n

While Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s base NP gain is nothing out of the ordinary, she possesses two boosts in the form of Mystic Eyes of Distortion\xe2\x80\x99s NP gain buff and Territory Creation\xe2\x80\x99s Arts performance buff. Furthermore, as an Archer, she comes with a naturally high Star Weight, and through Clairvoyance (Darkness), she is even able to provide her own stars should the situation call for it. This critical compatibility not only improves her NP gain, but also her overall damage.

\n
\n

Weaknesses

\n
\n

Low Availability

\n

At the time of writing, she has not had a second banner even in the Japanese version of the game. This means that Masters seeking to maximize her NP level can only do so within a limited amount of period, hampering her long-term value in comparison to her peers.

\n

Vulnerable to High-Damage Attacks

\n

Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s flat damage reduction is a type of buff which works best against weaker attacks, but high damaging hits such as NPs turn it next to useless. Despite having a 1-HP Guts to help, it is generally still the best idea to bring along a defensive support to handle such attacks.

\n

Below Average Damage Ceiling

\n

Aside from the aforementioned low availability, Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s lower Buster performance buff (35% against the normal 50%) makes her NP hit a bit weaker compared to other Servants, though being a single-target NP, this is generally not an issue against most enemies. It should be noted, however, that the damage boost lies on the same skill as her NP gain buff, which means that some amount of prior planning may be required before using it (though their 3-turn duration generally makes this an easy task).

\n

Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s first and third skills are very strong, and should be maxed as soon as possible. Meanwhile, her second can generally be left at a lower level, though leveling it is still ideal to increase her critical consistency.

\n
  • \n

    Mystic Eyes of Distortion (Skill) EX is by far the most packed of Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s skills. Containing her primary damage boost as well as the valuable NP gain buff and ignore defense (all of which last for 3 turns), maxing this skill grants her a significant offensive boost, something Fujino desperately needs. This skill should be leveled first.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n

    While both of Clairvoyance (Darkness) C\xe2\x80\x99s effects are fairly valuable to Fujino, only one of those (instant critical stars) scale with additional levels. This, combined with the situational nature of its sure hit effect, gives it the lowest leveling priority compared to her other skills.

    \n
    • \n
  • Despite having a Max HP reduction demerit, Residual Pain A is a very powerful sustain-oriented defensive skill. At max level, it deducts 2000 from any incoming damage, which makes it excellent against lower-damage normal attacks (or those softened by other Servants\xe2\x80\x99 defensive buffs). Furthermore, it also applies a Guts effect for 3 turns, letting her survive with 1 HP should something go wrong. This skill should be maxed second, to maximize its benefit and consistency.
    • \n

\n
\n
\n
\n

While Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s NP gain is far from being bad, she still lacks an instant NP charge of her own; as such, Starting NP Gauge CEs will generally be her go-to pick, ensuring the availability of Mystic Eyes of Distortion for her NP turn. Otherwise, she is best equipped with damage boosting CEs, such as those with NP Damage Up, Attack Up, or Buster Performance Up.

\n
  • \n

    Kaleidoscope / The Imaginary Element: These CEs give the most Starting NP compared to other choices, and are excellent for situations where an early buff block is needed. Even outside of those, they are still very strong options, lessening her reliance on external NP Charging supports.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n

    Aerial Drive / Holy Night Supper / Golden Sumo: Boulder Tournament: In a well-supported team, these CEs are Fujino\xe2\x80\x99s preferred choices, granting her a mixture of early NP and long-term damage boosts.

    \n
    • \n
  • The Black Grail: As with most damage dealers, The Black Grail gives Fujino the highest amount of overall damage increase. Furthermore, Residual Pain\xe2\x80\x99s Max HP reduction also makes the Black Grail\xe2\x80\x99s penalty somewhat redundant.
